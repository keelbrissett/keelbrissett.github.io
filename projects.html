DS1: Need Finding

In this assignment I planned and executed a round of needfinding to explore a technology of my choice related to personal health. I then created a Medium blog post to present my interview plan, data gathered from my interviews, and key insights obtained from the needfinding process.

DS2: Designing for Others

In this assignment we redesigned the Williams College Study Abroad website to cater to the needs of International Students at the college. This user-group was known only by our team, and we presented our proposal to the class. We then created a Medium Blog detailing our work

DS3: Designing for Interactivity

The goal of this design sprint was to practice selecting the right interaction mechanisms, namely gesture inputs. For this design sprint, each team chose an existing game or application on the web and then remapped the controls from keyboard/mouse to gesture inputs using the MediaPipe Gesture Recognizer library.

DS4: Designing for Effectiveness

In this design sprint, we practiced our web development skills and learned how to plan and run an evaluation to test the usability of a piece of software. Specifically, we redesigned a basic bookstore catalog website using the Bootstrap UI.

DS5: Accessibility Audits

In this assignment, I practiced thinking about design from an accessibility standpoint, finding trustworthy sources for how to improve accessibility, and translating accessibility requirements into practical changes to an interface.
